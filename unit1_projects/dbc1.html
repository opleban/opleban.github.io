In the beginning...

My name is Ori Jonathan Pleban. I was born and raised in the Central Valley of California. Eager to leave the area, I headed out east to Philadelphia where I did my undergraduate at the University of Pennsylvania.

After graduating college, I joined the Peace Corps and spent the last two years in Morocco living in a small mountain village nestled in the Middle Atlas Mountains. During my time in Morocco, I saw how technology and internet were affecting peoples lives in even the most rural parts of the countries.
